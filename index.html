My Story

My journey into web development started with curiosity and quickly turned into a passion for building products that solve real problems. Rather than simply learning programming languages, I focused on creating projects that people could actually use.

Today, I am pursuing CA Foundation while studying B.Com (Hons), balancing academics with software development. My biggest project, Asprients, is an educational platform designed to help students prepare smarter through organized resources, authentication, premium features, and an admin dashboard.

My goal is to combine business knowledge with technology to build impactful digital products that simplify learning and solve meaningful problems.
